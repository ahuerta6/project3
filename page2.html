Video Games

Valorant

Valorant is a first person shooter set in the future. Each person on each team have special abilities they are called "heroes". The game involves two teams of 5. Your objective on your team is to work together to beat the other team, like any other competetive game.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a Battle Royal based game where multiple teams of three drop on a map to see who will be the last team standing. Each team have pre-picked characters called "Legends" which have special abilities that help their team win the game. The role of each character varies but it is important to pick the characters that work well together in order to have a higher chance to succeed.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a game where you can create and destroy various types of blocks. There are two modes survival, and creative. In survival mode players must explore the map and get their own materials to survive. In creative mode you can do anything you want you don't need to find materials because you have unlimited supplies of everything, and you can't be killed by monsters.
